Significant Differences

Testing Significant Differences Between Proportions at 95% Confidence Level

This tool tests significance between sample proportions. To quote the Datastar website:

"Confidence levels computed provide the probability that a difference at least as large as noted would have occurred by chance if the two population proportions were in fact equal."

Just like the Datastar tool (using a pooled sample approach), we are running 2 proportion Z-tests, by taking sample size and percentages.

This tool differs from the Datastar tool, as it allows users to test tables of proportions against each other.
